    RecSys Tutorials¶

    The Recommendation Systems Course¶

    Master recommendation systems from classical collaborative filtering to Meta's HSTU and LLM-powered agents. 101 hands-on notebooks with production insights from Meta, Tencent, ByteDance, and Alibaba.

    Curriculum¶

    Learn the complete recommendation pipeline, from theory to production.

    Part 1: Foundations RecSys problem formulation, collaborative filtering, matrix factorization, embeddings, feature engineering, and the modern multi-stage pipeline. 8 notebooks

    Part 2: CTR Prediction From LR/FTRL to DeepFM, DIN, DIEN, MMoE, and PLE. The core ranking models powering every feed and ad system. 8 notebooks

    Part 3: Retrieval Two-tower models, ANN search with Faiss, multi-interest retrieval (MIND), RQ-VAE semantic IDs, TIGER, and generative retrieval. 9 notebooks

    Part 4: Advanced Ranking DCN-V2, SIM for long user histories, knowledge distillation, multi-scenario models, and multi-objective optimization. 8 notebooks

    Part 5: Sequential & Graph SASRec, BERT4Rec, LightGCN, knowledge graphs, contrastive learning, and scalable GNN training at Pinterest scale. 8 notebooks

    Part 6: Generative RecSys VAE, diffusion, LLM-as-recommender, HSTU (Meta), conversational rec, and multimodal recommendation. The paradigm shift. 10 notebooks

    Part 7: Agents & Interactive Reinforcement learning, LLM agents, multi-agent systems, tool-augmented recommendation, and feedback loop dynamics. 8 notebooks

    Part 8: Industrial Embeddings Trillion-scale embedding tables, compression techniques, feature stores, ID-free systems, and privacy-preserving features. 8 notebooks

    Part 9: Training Infra Distributed training, TorchRec, online learning, mixed-precision, and cost-efficient training at scale. 8 notebooks

    Part 10: Production Serving architecture, A/B testing, monitoring. Deep dives into Meta, Tencent, ByteDance, and Alibaba production stacks. 8 notebooks

    Part 11: Evaluation & Fairness Counterfactual evaluation, bias detection, fairness constraints, causal inference, and trustworthy recommendation. 8 notebooks

    Part 12: Research Frontiers Foundation models, RAG for rec, world models, on-device rec, and a comprehensive capstone project. 10 notebooks
